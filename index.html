Garfenomics

About the Game

Garfenomics is a game designed to teach people about some cyber security principles that may impact them in real world situations. In this game you are placed in the position of someone who is starting their own company, and must deal with the various cyber threats associated with this task. The currency in this case is Garfcoin, a fictional cryptocurrency.
