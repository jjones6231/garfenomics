Garfenomics

Garfenomics is a game designed to teach people about some cyber security principles that may impact them in real world situation. In this game you are placed in the position of someone who is starting their own company, and must deal with the various cyber treats associated with this task. The curraency in this case is Garfcoin, a fictional cryptocurrency.

How to play

You play the game by clicking on the picture of garfield to generate some initial currency. From there you can purchase upgrades from the right side of the screen which influence your passive income. Starting at various times, you will have a chance of getting hacked, to prevent these hacks you must purchase security upgrades from the left column. These upgrades are unlocked by purchasing security experts.
